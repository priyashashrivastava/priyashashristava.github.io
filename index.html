What I do

I expedite, simplify & add value to the process of establishing your digital footprint.
This can be achieved by performing a set of activities which are part of my forté.

  • In-depth Research & Analysis

    Analysis of the marketing data, past campaigns & a comprehensive competitor analysis to formulate the right strategy.

  • Strategizing & Media Planning

    Identifying the need for the creation of new assets like blogs, videos, creative social media posts, etc to boost your digital presence.

  • Promotion & Execution

    Execution of the strategy through owned, earned & paid channels for extensive reach.

  • Optimization

    Optimizing the campaigns, monitoring & boosting the performance to achieve the best results.

  • Budgeting

    Planning budget for financial expenditures on the most profitable media channels in line with the brand.

  • Leading & Co-ordinating

    Managing teams like Creative Designers, Website Developers, Content Writers, SEO, PPC, Public Relations & Call Support teams for executing the plan.

  • Improvisation

    Utilizing your existing digital assets to increase traffic & engaging the right audience.

  • Auditing & Reporting

    Evaluating the campaign progress & directing changes for efficiency & effectiveness which in return, impacts positively on expenditures.
